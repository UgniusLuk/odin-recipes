Grilled Cheese

Description

A sandwich with metled cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread

  • Cheese

  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan

  2. Add butter

  3. Put a couple of slices of cheese in between two peices of bread

  4. After butter melts, put the sandwhich on the pan and press down with a spatula

  5. After one side is golden brown, flip over and add butter and repeat previous step
